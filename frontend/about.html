About HealthBridge 🤝

HealthBridge is a collaborative digital platform designed to connect NGOs, healthcare professionals, volunteers, and patients into one unified ecosystem. Born out of our firsthand experiences during a social internship, this platform simplifies the organization and management of healthcare drives—making community outreach more efficient, structured, and accessible to those who need it most.
