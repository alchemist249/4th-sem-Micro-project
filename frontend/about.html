About HealthBridge

HealthBridge is a collaborative digital platform designed to connect NGOs, healthcare professionals, volunteers, and patients in one unified ecosystem 🤝. Our goal is to simplify the organization and management of healthcare drives, making outreach more efficient, structured, and accessible to those who need it most. Through streamlined coordination and clear communication channels, HealthBridge helps bridge the gap between healthcare providers and communities in need 🌍. This project is a continuation of our social internship experience from the previous year, where we witnessed firsthand the challenges in organizing medical camps and awareness drives. Built with that insight, HealthBridge transforms those learnings into a practical solution that enhances real-world impact. This initiative is developed by Amrit Chetri, Ashal Pradhan, and Mohammad Oliulla, reflecting our shared commitment to social welfare and technology-driven healthcare solutions 💡.
